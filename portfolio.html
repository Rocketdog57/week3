My name is Andrew Garnett, I am a disabled USAF veteran. I served in the military for eight year and received my honorable discharge in May 1983. Since leaving the military I have received a degree in Business Administration and I owned two restaurants before becoming disabled.
I am currently exploring my options as a web developer and is enrolled in a course at the Clubhou.se. I have been active in politics as a candidate and the establishment of a political party. I co-founder the first black chamber of commerce in city of augusta and the state of Ga.
